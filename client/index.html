About Me

Hello! I'm Sarah Bannerman, an aspiring potter and ceramic artist who loves to create unique and beautiful pieces of pottery. I specialize in creating functional pieces that are both aesthetically pleasing and practical. I have been creating pottery for over 10 years, and I have developed my own unique style and technique that I use to create each piece. I also enjoy experimenting with different glazes and finishes to create unique and one-of-a-kind pieces. I'm passionate about creating beautiful and functional pottery, and I'm excited to share my work with you!
